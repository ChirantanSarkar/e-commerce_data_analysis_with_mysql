# Blinkit E-Commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a Blinkit e-commerce database using MySQL.

The analysis covers
* Customer behavior
* Sales performance,
* Inventory management
* Delivery operations
* Customer feedback
* Marketing campaign effectiveness.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

# Business Findings

## 1. Customer Analysis

* The platform has **2,500 customers** who placed a total of **5,000 orders**, averaging **2 orders per customer**.
* Customer segmentation is evenly distributed:

  * **Regular Customers:** 639
  * **Premium Customers:** 633
  * **New Customers:** 628
  * **Inactive Customers:** 600
* The balanced distribution indicates that the customer base is diversified, reducing dependence on any single customer segment.
* **Pooja Mital** emerged as the highest-spending customer with an average order value of **₹1,999.83**, closely followed by several other high-value customers.
* Multiple customers recorded **20 orders each**, indicating a group of highly engaged repeat buyers who could be targeted through loyalty and retention programs.

___

## 2. Sales Performance

* The platform generated **₹11009308.50 (₹11.01 million)** in total revenue from **5,000 completed orders**.
* The **Average Order Value (AOV)** of **₹2,201.86** suggests customers frequently purchase multiple products or higher-value items.

___

## 3. Product and Inventory Analysis

* The business manages a catalog of **268 products** across multiple categories.
* Inventory records indicate:

  * **147,526 units** of stock received.
  * **80,268 units** recorded as damaged stock.
* Damaged inventory accounts for a substantial portion of stock, highlighting the need to review warehouse handling, storage and quality control.
* Monitoring stock replenishment and minimizing inventory losses can significantly improve operational efficiency and profit margin.

___

## 4. Delivery Performance

* The average delivery time is **4.44**
* Delivery status analysis shows:

  * **On-Time Deliveries:** 3,470 (69.4%)
  * **Slightly Delayed Deliveries:** 1,037 (20.7%)
  * **Significantly Delayed Deliveries:** 493 (9.9%)
* Nearly **7 out of every 10 orders** were delivered on time, demonstrating generally reliable delivery performance.
* The leading causes of delivery delays were:

  * **Traffic:** 3,098 cases
  * **Unknown:** 1,902 cases
* Traffic congestion appears to be the primary operational challenge affecting delivery efficiency.

___

## 5. Customer Feedback Analysis

* Customers provided an average satisfaction rating of **3.34 out of 5**, indicating a moderate level of overall satisfaction.
* Sentiment analysis shows:

  * **Positive Feedback:** 1,620
  * **Neutral Feedback:** 1,738
  * **Negative Feedback:** 1,642
* Customer opinions are relatively balanced, suggesting opportunities to improve the overall shopping experience.
* Feedback categories are evenly distributed:

  * Delivery: 1,271
  * Customer Service: 1,266
  * Product Quality: 1,250
  * App Experience: 1,213
* Since these categories share a common customer feedback, improving logistics, customer service and maintaining a product standard could have a meaningful impact on customer satisfaction.

___

## 6. Marketing Performance

* Total marketing investment amounted to **₹16.32 million**.
* Marketing campaigns generated **₹32.19 million** in revenue.
* The overall **Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS)** is approximately **1.97**, meaning every **₹1 invested in marketing generated approximately ₹1.97 in revenue**.
* The highest-performing campaigns included:

  * Weekend Special
  * Festival Offer
  * Category Promotion
  * Referral Program
  * New User Discount
* These campaigns consistently generated revenues close to **₹10,000 per campaign**, indicating effective promotional performance.

___

# Key Business Insights

* The platform maintains a healthy customer base with consistent purchasing activity.
* Revenue generation is strong, supported by a relatively high average order value.
* Marketing campaigns produce positive returns, with nearly double the revenue compared to advertising spend.
* Delivery performance is generally strong, and also reducing traffic-related delays could further improve service quality.
* Customer satisfaction is moderate, indicating opportunities to enhance delivery, product quality, and customer service.

___

# Recommendations

### Customer Retention

Introduce personalized loyalty programs and targeted promotions for repeat customers and premium customer segments.

### Inventory Optimization

Investigate the root causes of damaged inventory and strengthen warehouse handling, packaging and quality assurance.

### Delivery Improvements

Optimize delivery routes, improve traffic management strategies, and monitor delivery partner performance to reduce delays.

### Customer Experience

Focus on improving delivery quality, customer support, and product consistency to increase customer satisfaction ratings and reduce negative feedback.

### Marketing Strategy

Allocate more resources toward high-performing campaigns such as **Weekend Special**, **Festival Offer**, and **Category Promotion**, while continuously monitoring ROAS  (Return on Advertising Spend) to maximize marketing efficiency.

___

# Conclusion

The findings show that the business has a strong customer base, effective marketing campaigns, and healthy revenue generation.

However, improvements in inventory management, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction can further enhance operational performance and long-term profitability.
